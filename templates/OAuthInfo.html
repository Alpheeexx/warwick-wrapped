We require a sign-in with Warwick ITS to access your Tabula information. When you sign in with your Warwick ITS account, this gives us permission to view and process your data in line with our privacy policy, and is required for the functioning of our site.

Q: Do we receive your Warwick username and password?

A: No, we cannot view your username and password. When you log in with ITS, we receive a token providing permission to view your Tabula data. This token is used in place of a username and password, but can only be used to make Tabula requests.

Q: How can I remove permission?

A: Permission to access the information can be revoked by removing the authorised token at https://websignon.warwick.ac.uk/oauth/tokens.
